About Us

Welcome To CIGA sarl

CIGA sarl is a diversified group of companies. It is also an agency for business promotions and asset management. Its builds the future of the economic middle class of the Democratic Republic of Congo and Africa.

DRC have become the most wanted investment destination in Central Africa because of its potential for development. Note that every economic sector in DRC still have to be developed. While promoting the positive image of the democratic republic of Congo and its Business opportunities to the local and foreign entrepreneurs and investors; we also negotiate Placements and business deals to the DRC government and private companies for our partners.

IN SHORT, WE ARE THE GATEWAY OF THE OFFER AND THE DEMAND FOR THE DRC MARKET.

We are the bridge between Entrepreneurs in the DRC and those of the rest of the world.

Contact Us

OUR VISION

CIGA SARL wants a DRC whose economy is dynamic, innovative, sustainable and characterized by growth, employment and fair opportunities, fully exploiting the potential of each.

Our vision is to become the solution to the common problem the majority of African face. “POVERTY”

We want to be the largest platform for entrepreneurs and an excellent gateway for foreign investors in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to build the DRC's entrepreneurial ecosystem by:Promoting the DRC as a destination for foreign investment; Stimulating the development and growth of companies; Providing a support mechanism for business development;Establishing investment culture.

OUR OBJECTIVE

Our objective is jobs and wealth creation of on the African continent in general and especially in the Democratic Republic of Congo in particular.

We believe that the time has come for Africa to emerge and take the lead in the global economy. CIGA sarl is the solution to the problem linked to poverty and unemployment.
